## 🔨 NEW LMIA RULES (Effective April 1, 2026)

### MAJOR CHANGES for Employer Clients:

**1. Advertising Period DOUBLED:**
- **Old:** 4 weeks
- **NEW:** 8 consecutive weeks
- Must stay active until ESDC decides
- **Impact:** 12-16 weeks total timeline (vs 8-12 weeks before)

**2. Youth Recruitment MANDATORY:**
- Employers MUST target ages 15-30
- Must show concrete recruitment efforts
- **Your action:** Update LMIA prep checklists

**3. Rural Workforce Cap:**
- Increased to **15%** (from 10%)
- Only in rural communities
- Valid until March 31, 2027

**Compliance:**
- Must advertise on 3+ platforms (including Job Bank)
- Must include wage, location, duties, qualifications
